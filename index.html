Who is this for?

LeadLoop is aimed at service-based small and medium businesses that want a more helpful website experience without building custom software from scratch.

Is this product live yet?

Not yet. This page is for validating interest, gathering early adopters, and learning what use cases matter most before investing in the full hosted product.

What would the first version likely focus on?

The strongest early use cases are answering common questions, capturing leads, and helping visitors move toward appointments or contact actions.
